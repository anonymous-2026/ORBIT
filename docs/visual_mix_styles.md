# Visual Mix Styles

ORBIT exposes exactly three visual mix styles.

## IFRO

`IFRO` means **In-Frame Relational Overlay**. It draws boxes, semantic IDs, and relation arrows inside the policy image.

Use it when spatial relation visibility is more important than preserving a minimally modified image.

## SSP

`SSP` means **Sidecar Semantic Panel**. It keeps the image lightly marked with IDs and places semantic details in a side panel.

Use it for short implicit-intent tasks where text disambiguation matters and dense in-frame annotations could obscure objects.

## DCSM

`DCSM` means **Dual-Channel Semantic Mix**. It combines IFRO-style relation marks with the SSP side panel.

Use it for cluttered multi-object tasks where both spatial marking and semantic role explanation may be necessary.
